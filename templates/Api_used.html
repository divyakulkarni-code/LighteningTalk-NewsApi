Method Used

all_articles = newsapi.get_everything(q='Corona Virus', language='en', page=2)

Data Structure

  • type(all_articles) --> {}
  • all_articles.keys() ----> (['status','totalResults','articles']
  • articles --- > [list of all web articles]
  • all_articles['articles'][0] ---- > {key : value}

Request Parameters

  • q - Keywords or phrases to search for in the article title and body.
  • sources - for the news sources or blogs you want headlines from
  • domains - bbc.co.uk, techcrunch.com, engadget.com
  • from/to : A date and optional time
  • sortby : popularity, relavancy, or published at
